Problem 2

polymer. Unfortunately, you cannot do such an experiment because the sample is too viscous,
so it overloads the transducer and comes out of the cone (or plate) and plate gap. Suggest two
other experiments you could do that would provide the same information as viscosity versus
shear rate. Explain how the material functions of these experiments relate to steady state
viscosity.

0.01 and 100 rad/s and you have no access to other machines. Explain how you can get this
data without resorting to other instruments. Use diagrams or sketches to help illustrate your
point.
