SUMMARY

1D:
‚ÄĘ The diad and reflection are the only symmetry operations allowed
in 1D lattices
‚ÄĘ There is one linear Bravais lattice
‚ÄĘ Combination of reflection and lattice translation creates a glide line
for the basis
‚ÄĘ There are in total of 6 line groups in 1D
2D:
‚ÄĘ The diad, triad, tetrad, hexad and reflection are the only symmetry
operations allowed in 2D lattices
‚ÄĘ There are 5 plane Bravais lattices
‚ÄĘ Reflection and lattice translation create a glide line
‚ÄĘ There are in total 17 plane groups in 2D
General:
‚ÄĘ The line and space groups encode the Bravais lattice, the plane
group and the principal symmetry elements consistent with the
