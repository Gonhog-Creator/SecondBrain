Self-Similarity of Critical Gel



At the gel point, the system is self-similar – a statistical fractal: M ~ R df



1989)



For n = 0.13, we get a fractal dimension df = 2.38



High df indicates a dense fractal (due to high polymer MW, filler crosslinks)
