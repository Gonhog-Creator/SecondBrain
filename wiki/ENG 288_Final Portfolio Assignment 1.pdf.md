## Full Content
Final Portfolio Assignment
The final portfolio assignment is worth about 30% of your final grade, and it CANNOT
be turned in late. This assignment is due at 11:59 PM on April 25, 2025.
Your final portfolio MUST include at least at least 20 total pages (double-spaced, 12
pt Times New Roman font).
You may submit any page combination of fiction writing. For example, you
may choose to submit 20, 1-page flash fiction pieces. Or, perhaps, a 15-page short story
and a 5-page flash piece. Etc. Etc.
You may choose to submit revisions from workshop. If you are submitting a
revision, it should be a substantive revision—meaning, you are not to copy &
paste something you submitted to workshop this semester without incorporating the
feedback from your colleagues. You may also choose to submit something that has never
seen the light of day before, something that no one before me (your instructor) has read.
Most importantly, please submit your best work, work that you are proud
of. The parameters of this assignment are broad so that you can experiment and create
freely. Remember: What I think about your writing (content, style, etc.) will not impact
your final grade. Just get to 20 pages.
***Images do not count towards page count but can be included if it supplements your
writing. Please email me if you are thinking about including other multimedia
elements that may be equivalent in time to writing 20 pages but may affect your page
count (think multi-media, etc).
