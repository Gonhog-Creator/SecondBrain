Overview
Congratulations on your internship with Wolfspeed, we are so excited for you to start! All
interns are required to complete a background check and a drug screen prior to their start
date. You will soon be sent an email from noreply@sterling.app asking you to authorize a
background check.
This background link will expire after 5 calendar days, so please do this as soon as you can so
we can ensure all parts of your background check pass compliance before your start date. If
all components of your background check don’t come through “cleared” in time, we will have to
push your start date back.

Signing in
Create an Account – The first time a candidate logs into Sterling they will be required to
create an account by entering an email address and creating a password (must be at least 8
characters in length with 1 uppercase and lowercase letter, 1 number, and 1 special
character). Candidate will need to confirm the password in the next field and agree to
Sterling’s Terms of User by marking the checkbox. To officially create the account, candidate
must select “Create Account”.
Returning Users – If a candidate has already logged into Sterling they will need to select “I am
a returning user” located at the bottom on the initial invite screen below the “Create
Account” button. This will load a new screen where the candidate will need to enter their
email and password and then select “Log in”.

Welcome Screen
Once logged in candidate will see a welcome screen with the Wolfspeed logo located at the
top center of the screen. There will be a welcome message explaining to the candidates what
to expect when completing the process. Once this has been read and reviewed candidate
should select “Lets Get Started”.

Scheduler- Drug Screen
One of the final steps candidates will need to complete prior to submitting their order is
scheduling their drug screen. This tab will autofill the zip code field to the current address
that the candidate entered in the “My Information” tab. This can be altered to better suit the
candidate’s needs.
Below the zip code field, a list of test sites will appear for the candidate to choose from.
Please note that the hours of operation are listed below each site. Once a testing site is
selected, candidate will need to select “Next” (located at the bottom right of the screen).

Misc. Drug Screening information - It is always best practice to do your drug screen as soon
as you are able. A typical drug screen may take anywhere from 3-10 business days to come
back.

Review
The final screen candidates will need to look over is the “Review” tab. This should be
thoroughly checked by the candidate. Once this step have been completed, candidate will
need to select “Confirm” (located at the bottom right of the screen).
Note: Orders will not be officially submitted until the candidate has selected confirm. A
confirmation email with drug screening location details will also be sent (if applicable) within a
few minutes. If candidate does not receive this confirmation email, they should reach out to
Sterling as soon as possible.
