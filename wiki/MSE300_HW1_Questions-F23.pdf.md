Q5) Crystal structure visualization: (55 pts)
The unit cell of an oxide crystal with a cubic lattice parameter a = 4.00 Å contains
the following atoms (the ordered sets are the coefficients of location that define rj):
A at (0,0,0); B at (1/2 , 1/2, 1/2);
O at (1/2, 1/2, 0); O at (0, 1/2, 1/2); O at (1/2, 0, 1/2).
a) Specify the Bravais lattice of this crystal.
b) Specify the basis of this crystal.
c) Specify the formula unit of this compound.
d) Sketch the unit cell (perspective view), including all atoms that are within or
partially within the cell, making sure to replicate atoms at corners, edges
and facets, as needed. (hint: use different colors or patterns to identify
atoms)
e) What are the coordination numbers of A and B?
f) Determine the A-O and B-O bond lengths in Å.
g) If A and B are not the same size, which is smaller? Explain your rationale?
h) Specify the sequence of atoms found along the <111> and <110>
directions.
