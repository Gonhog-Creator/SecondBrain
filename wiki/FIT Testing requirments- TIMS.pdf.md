The Participant hereby expressly acknowledges and agrees as follows:
2.1.

General Manufacturing Location Requirements

2.1.1.

As described in Section 4.3 of NIJ CTP Conformity Assessment Document 101-07, Participants shall notify the NIJ CTP of all active manufacturi
locations and where NIJ-compliant body armor final assembly takes place. The Participant agrees to comply with the requirements of Section

2.1.2.

This agreement does not authorize any changes except the manufacturing location and the associated information regarding an authorized
manufacturing location appearing on the label.

2.1.3.

The body armor model identified in this model application shall be manufactured only at the location(s) identified in this model application.

2.1.3.1.

If there are changes to (e.g., additional locations, change of physical location of a manufacturing location, start or termination of productio
at a location), the Participant shall notify the NIJ CTP prior to these changes taking effect.

2.1.3.2.

Any other changes to manufacturing locations (e.g., production is halted at a designated manufacturing location for any reason) shall be
communicated to the NIJ CTP as soon as possible.

2.1.3.2.1.

Notification that production at a specific location has stopped may take up to 30 days for the NIJ CTP to process and stop surveillance
activities.

2.1.3.3.

Failure of the Participant to provide current and accurate information concerning manufacturing locations could result in unnecessary cos
the Participant in the form of unnecessary surveillance (i.e., FIT) charges.

2.1.3.4.

Failure of the Participant, additional Listee(s) or locations to abide by the requirements of the NIJ CTP could have a negative impact on the
Participant’s continued participation in the NIJ CTP, up to and including the removal of all Participant models from the NIJ Compliant
Products List (CPL) under any brand or trademark.

2.3.

Access to Manufacturing Locations for Surveillance Activities (Follow-up Inspection and Testing)

2.3.1.

The Participant expressly acknowledges and agrees that full, safe, and secure access shall be provided to NIJ CTP staff, representatives, and th
agents or designees, either announced or unannounced, for all manufacturing and storage facilities and all records and personnel used in the
manufacture of models of equipment identified in this model application.

2.3.1.1.

This access includes the selection and removal of production samples from manufacturing facilities at the Participant’s expense for the
purposes of surveillance testing (FIT). Refer to Section 11.7.3 of NIJ CTP Document 101-07 for details on selection and handling of FIT test
items.

2.3.2.

The point of contact (or alternate) for each location shall be available on site and prepared to escort the Inspection Body representative during
on-site inspection.

2.3.3.

The Participant may verify that NIJ CTP staff, representatives, or their agents or designees comply with any U.S. Persons requirements (i.e.,
citizenship status) applied to its own employees regarding access to business confidential, proprietary, or trade secret information or data prio
their entry.

2.3.3.1.

The Participant is responsible for communicating the manufacturing location’s requirements to the NIJ CTP and the NIJ CTP–designated
Inspection Body prior to the first inspection to avoid unnecessary costs to the Participant resulting from an incomplete or canceled
inspection.
