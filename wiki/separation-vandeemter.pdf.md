B-term: Longitudinal Diffusion
B = 2 Dm

mobile phase.

A-term: Multiple Flow Paths
Eddy diffusion
• A depends on particle size; smaller particles give a smaller A.
• A is nearly zero for open tubular (OT) columns.
• At moderate to high flow rates, the A-term is independent of flow rate.
C-term: mass transfer terms
C = Cm + Cs
Cm: a component to C in the mobile phase
Cs: a component to C in the stationary phase.
The C term dominates a high flow rates.
