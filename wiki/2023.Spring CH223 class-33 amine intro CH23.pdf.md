Acidity of Amines
•
•
•

NH3, RNH2, and R2NH are amphoteric: they may act as bases and acids.
They are very weakly acidic (unstable conjugate base).
The conjugate base of an amine is called an amide (do not confuse with
amide derivatives of carboxylic acids).
