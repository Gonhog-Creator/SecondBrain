Example 1. It has been established over many years of operation that the daily production from a
certain small oil refinery is a normally-distributed variate with an average production of 19,425
barrels of oil per day (bbl/day) and a standard deviation of 500 bbl/day. (a) Determine the
probability that on a random day the refinery will produce at most 19,680 bbl/day. (b) Determine
the probability that on a random day the refinery will produce at least 19,680 bbl/day. (c) What is
the probability that the oil refinery will produce between 19,000 and 20,000 barrels of oil on a
randomly-selected day of operation? (d) What is the 95% confidence interval for daily oil
production from the refinery?
