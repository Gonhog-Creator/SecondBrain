Seven Reactor SCRAM’s:
1. Linear Power Channel SCRAM at
> 120% Full Power (1MW)
2. Safety Power Channel SCRAM at
> 120% Full Power (1MW)
3. Primary Coolant Flow SCRAM at
< 475 GPM (enables if Log-N or
Safety Channel < 150 kW)
4. Flow Flapper Valve OPEN SCRAM
(enables if Log-N or Safety
Channel < 150 kW)

< 36” below full
