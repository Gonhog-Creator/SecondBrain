PROCEDURE:
1.

Bring the reactor critical at power levels as indicated on Table #1 referencing the 16N Power Channel.

2.

Allow temperatures to stabilize for 10 minutes prior to taking temperature data.

3.

Record temperature values on Table #1 as indicated on the following RTD’s (Resistance Temperature
Detectors):
T2 – Pool Temperature (RTD is ~6 feet above top of fuel assemblies)
T3 – Primary Cold (Return to pool)
T4 – Primary Hot (Core outlet)
T5 – Heat Exchanger (Hot) Primary Inlet
T6 – Heat Exchanger (Cold) Primary Return
T7 – Heat Exchanger (Cold) Secondary Inlet (from cooling tower condensate basin)
T8 – Heat Exchanger (Hot) Secondary Outlet (to cooling tower spray header)

4.

Measure Core ∆T and calculate reactor power (kW) using the mass flow equation above.
