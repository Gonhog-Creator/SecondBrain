NIH U-Team Fellowship Application
11/10/22
Jose Barbeito
While I am unsure of what this letter is supposed to explain, I will attempt to give a brief
overview of my desire for the NIH Fellowship. I have always been interested in doing any type of
research, and biomedical research specifically is always on the table as an interest of mine. My
father is a doctor and the importance of medicine in keeping the public healthy and functioning
is extremely important, as seen with the Coronavirus pandemic that crippled the entire world.
Taking HON 367 last year with Mrs. Veale gave me a quick glimpse into the world of biomedical
engineering and biomedical research and got me wondering how I could potentially enter this
area. Studying chemical engineering, the skills I gain in doing biomedical research would greatly
benefit my future career, of that I am confident. I hope to add the minor to my classwork and
become involved in the NIH training program and help with research that I am passionate about.
