1.

Bring the reactor critical and establish critical gang rod positions at a reactor power of 1 kilowatt (kW).

2.

Withdraw control rods and place the reactor on a positive period of not less than 15 seconds.

3.

Allow power to increase until the desired power level is reached (see Table #1 on next page) and then insert
rods to reestablish criticality.

4.

Establish criticality at each of the power levels given on Table #1 and record the Gang Rod Position
(inches) at each power level.

5.

Repeat steps #2 through #4 until a reactor power of 1000kW is reached.
