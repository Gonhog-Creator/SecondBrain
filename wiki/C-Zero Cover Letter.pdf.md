Jose M. Barbeito
919-667-4663 · jbarbei@ncsu.edu

josebarbeito.com
January 8th, 2023
To whom it may concern,
I am writing to apply for the Chemical Engineering Intern position in Goleta, CA seeking
chemical engineers. I believe that my interest in chemical engineering, passion for decarbonizing
the future, and quick learning make me an ideal candidate for this position.
Of the many projects I have worked on, the one that most aligns with this position would be my
synthesis of liquid bromine from a solution of acids and bromides. Inspired from organic
chemistry reactions and online sources, I independently planned, researched, and assembled the
process and required components. Working with gasses and extracting the liquid bromine via
distillation and the purification from the mixture was a valuable experience that helped me
develop skills for handling toxic liquids and gasses, utilizing proper lab PPE, and controlling
reaction rates.
I have always had a passion for decarbonizing the future, pushing my family and friends towards
clean energy and keeping informed on the latest technologies. When I first learned about C-Zero
last year, it was the company that intrigued me the most due to its ability to be adapted to our
current infrastructure and its potential to be connected to other clean energy sources for
maximum efficiency.
Alongside my interest and skills, I am an avid learner, working great in stimulating environments
that teach me new things. I also have plenty of experience working with machines, whether it be
simple power tools or lab equipment. Put together, I believe I am a strong candidate for this
position, and would be ecstatic to join the team.

Thank you for your consideration.
Sincerely,
Jose Barbeito
