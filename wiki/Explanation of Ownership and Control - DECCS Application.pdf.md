Explanation of Ownership and Control​
Delta V International, LLC
Delta V International, LLC is a limited liability company organized under the laws of the State of
North Carolina.
The ownership structure is as follows:
●​ Jose Maria Barbeito, a U.S. citizen, owns 50% of the Company​
●​ Fernando Barbeito, a citizen of Argentina, owns 50% of the Company​

The Company is member-managed. Operational and strategic decisions are made by both
members.
The Company has foreign ownership through Fernando Barbeito. This individual is affiliated with
the following foreign entities:
●​ Delta V Blindajes(Argentina)​
●​ ASP Blindajes (Argentina)​

These entities are engaged in the manufacture of ballistic protective equipment. The U.S.
Company intends to act as an importer and distributor of such products.
No other persons or entities hold ownership or control in the Company.
