Benefits of Flexibility
• Increased production & Retention of

muscular injuries
• Slows down loss of joint mobility due to

athletics
• May prevent, delay and/or lessen pain
associated with arthritis
• Improves balance, stability and coordination
• Reduction in stiffness, tightness and
muscular tension
• Better neuromuscular coordination.
