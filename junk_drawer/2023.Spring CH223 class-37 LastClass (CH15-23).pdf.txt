Adding Hydrogen Halides to Dienes: 1,2- and 1,4Additions
Two types of additions can occur when conjugated dienes react with hydrogen
halides:
• 1,2-addition: Occurs at adjacent carbons.
• 1,4-addition: Occurs to carbons that have a 1,4-relationship.
