SPECIFICATIONS
The requirements described in these specifications are for minimum acceptable standards for
Ballistic Resistance Police Body Armor for the City of East Providence Police Department. The
intent is to purchase state-of-the-art Ballistic Resistance Police Body Armor with optimum
protection and wear-ability. All bidders shall adhere strictly to these requirements. A complete
justification for any deviation is to be furnished along with the City’s substitution form 72 hours
prior to the bid closing time and date.
ESTIMATED ANNUAL QUANTITIES
The City estimates that it will purchase fifteen (84) Ballistic Resistance Police Body Armor units
for officers over the next three years, approximately (44) in year one. Approximately (8) Ballistic
Resistance Police Body Armor units will be for female officers off this bid. These estimated
quantities are based upon the best available information. The City reserves the right to increase or
decrease the quantity by any amount deemed necessary to meet its needs without any adjustments
in the quoted price.

DESIGN REQUIREMENTS
The armor described herein is intended for primary use as an
undergarment.
PROTECTIVE COVERAGE
The armor must provide maximum practical protective coverage
for male and female officers for everyday wear. Armor provides
side protection for full torso coverage. Overlapping the front and
back panels by at least 1 inch—preferably 2 inches. NIJ suggests
that when overlapping the two panels, the front panel should
overlap the back panel to prevent a round from “skipping” between
the two panels.

14.
Suitable relief of one to two inches must be allowed between the
bottom of the vest and the gun belt while standing so that no
binding occurs when the officer is in the sitting position.
Ample relief must be provided in the armpit and neck region.
Specified thickness, flexibility, weight, and fit requirements are
intended to maximize everyday wear.
BALLISTIC VEST CARRIER
Each vest shall be supplied with two black six point adjustable
carriers designed to maximize moisture-wicking, evaporation and
temperature management such as GoreTex®
or CoolMax®,
15.
Coloring shall be permanent and not “bleed” onto other garments.
Small hook and loop (Velcro) closures are required.
The carrier must have shirttails.
16.

CUSTOM FIT
Each vest must be custom fitted and made to measurements. All
measurements must be made by factory-trained personnel.
Initial fit of custom measured vests must be guaranteed to fit
satisfactorily on delivery. The Bidder or manufacturer must agree
to provide alterations or replacement free until the wearer is
satisfied. This includes initial fit of both male and female vests.

18.
Manufacturer must provide adjustment for the chest, waist, and
shoulders with the minimum relief under arms, neck, and shoulder
necessary to prevent chafing of the wearer. Be designed in such a
manner as to prevent the armor from “riding up” on the wearer
during
normal duty activities. All closure, fastening, or accessory
attachment devices should be made of materials that do not present
a “secondary projectile” or “ricochet” hazard if struck by a bullet.

19.

PERFORMANCE WARRANTY
With normal use and no abuse, field ballistic performance to
specified threat level must be warranted for a minimum of five
years from the date of original purchase.
Outer carriers must be guaranteed against defects in workmanship
for a minimum of twelve months from the date of purchase.
