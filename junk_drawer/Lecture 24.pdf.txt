Example Problem
Consider the polymer below (MW > 10n6) with one side group sticking out for every two
repeat units of the backbone. There are two possible enzymes, one that breaks the side
groups and the other that cleaves the backbone randomly. (a) Your friend Joe claims that
using the back-bone cleaving enzyme would have the most effect on viscosity. Does this make
sense. Justify your answer. (b) Your other friend Bloe has used the side chain-cleaving
enzyme to cleave some side groups. He comes back after a 7-day vacation and measures the
viscosity of the enzymatically-modified sample and finds its viscosity higher than the
original sample. Explain if this result is possible and why, or if your friend is wrong and
why.
