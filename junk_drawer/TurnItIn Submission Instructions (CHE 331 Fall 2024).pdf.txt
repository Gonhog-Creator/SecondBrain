TurnItIn Submission Instructions (CHE 331 Fall 2024)
As stated in the course syllabus, CHE 331 laboratory reports will be screened for plagiarism
using the TurnItIn.com service. Please review this procedure for submitting your laboratory
reports to TurnItIn.com:
1. Select one member of your laboratory group to serve as the "contact person" for TurnItIn
submissions. This person will be responsible for all submissions to the service - students
within individual groups should not submit multiple copies of the same report.
2. Browse to www.turnitin.com
3. Click the Create Account link located in the upper-right of the page.
4. Under "Create New Account," select Student.
5. Enter in your name, etc. in the boxes located on the Create Account page. Use the
following log-in information specific for CHE 331:
1. Class ID: 44996769
2. Class Enrollment Password: FALL2024
6. Read the User Agreement, confirm your age and click the "I Agree" button.
7. The next page will show the TurnItIn homepage for the course. Click on the name of the
course.
8. The following page will show the assignments you will need to submit to TurnItIn over
the course of the semester. When ready to upload your report for an assignment, click the
Submit button located next to the particular assignment.
9. Upload the file containing your report by selecting Single File Upload from the dropdown box and selecting the desired file. Be sure to upload your report as a PDF file;
although TurnItIn.com accepts MS Word files, files submitted in formats other than PDF
can lead to formatting errors when viewed through TurnItIn.com. Any formatting errors
resulting from uploading a file in a format other than PDF will be considered errors in the
report and may result in point deductions.
10. On the following page, confirm via the preview window that you uploaded the correct file
and click Submit.
11. The next page will show a digital receipt of your submission to TurnItIn.
Additional information for student users is provided by TurnItIn on their website,
including tutorials on software use. Please email Dr. Kim Dickey if you have any questions
about using TurnItIn.
