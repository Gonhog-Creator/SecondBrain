Example
If the retention factor for a component is k=5, what fraction of time
does this component spend in the mobile phase?
k=5=(tr-tm)/tm = ts/tm
Fraction spent in mobile phase = tm/(tm+ts) =?

tm/(tm+ts) = tm/(tm+5tm) = tm/6tm = 1/6

respectively. All times were measured from injection. The two poorly
resolved peaks had base widths of 13.0 s and 15.0 s, respectively.
Calculate R for the two peaks and estimate the % resolution using the
normal distribution table.

R=Dt/wav = 2(90.0-70.0)/(13.0+15.0) =1.428..
z= 2R = 2.857.. ~ 2.9
